HOW TO RUN ON YOUR PHONE:
================================================================================

STEP 1: Enable USB Debugging
  1. Go to Settings
  2. About Phone (scroll to bottom)
  3. Find "Build Number" - tap 7 times
  4. Developer Options will appear
  5. Enable "USB Debugging"

STEP 2: Connect Phone
  1. Plug phone into computer with USB cable
  2. Allow USB debugging permission on phone

STEP 3: Install & Run
  Open command terminal and run:

  cd "C:\Users\Source Tech Co\AndroidStudioProjects\projectyear"

  Then uninstall old version:
  adb uninstall com.example.projectyear

  Then build and install:
  .\gradlew.bat clean assembleDebug
  adb install app/build/outputs/apk/debug/app-debug.apk

STEP 4: Launch App
  The app appears on your phone's home screen
  Tap to launch - NO CRASHES!
